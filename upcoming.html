Short Film

The motive of this event is to strengthen our relationships with family and friends during these days of pandemic.This time we are here to onset a competition event where participants should make a video featuring a spoof or a comedy or a moral.

Details are as follows:
• People with any age can participate as well as can prefer languages of their known.
• The duration of the video should be 5 minutes.
• Theme of the video: Spoof, Moral, Comedy (any one).
• Registration Fees: Rs.150
• Last day for registration: 8th July
• Video to be submitted by: 10th July

Enlightenment:

Here we give some instances about the themes so that the participants can get some general intentions and ideas.
1. A Spoof Video The video under this category should contain a drama acted by the participants about any scenario, which could be from a famous movie, a web series, a television serial, etc. Just for an instance “A scene from the movie Bahubali! “ Differently, the video can contain scenarios depicted by some famous characters, or acting of the particular character. Such as “The Professor from the series Money Heist!”
2. A Comedy Video The video under this category should contain a drama acted by the participants about any funny situation, scenario or an incident.
3.A Moral Video The video under this category should contain a drama acted by the participants about any incident, a story which gives a moral lesson to the viewers.

As we all know, the videos can only be shot inside our homes, which would be an amazing way to get together with our family and make the best creations. These little moments with our families become tomorrow's precious memories.
